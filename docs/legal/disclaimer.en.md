## Not a Medical Device

GlucoTray is **not a certified medical device** and has not been evaluated, approved, or cleared by any medical regulatory authority (including but not limited to the FDA, the EU's MDR, or equivalent bodies). GlucoTray is an independent hobby/companion project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any manufacturer of continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) systems or their companion apps.

## Not a Replacement for Your Official CGM App

GlucoTray is a **convenience display tool only**. It is designed for users who spend extended periods at a computer and wish to see their glucose value without checking their phone. GlucoTray retrieves data from third-party CGM cloud services (such as Dexcom Share, and potentially others in the future) but does **not** replace the official app provided by your CGM manufacturer. That official app must remain your primary source for glucose monitoring.

## No Alerts or Alarms

GlucoTray does **not** provide active alerts, alarms, sounds, or push notifications for high or low glucose values. The system tray icon only passively displays color-coded zones. GlucoTray must never be relied upon to notice or respond to a hypoglycemic or hyperglycemic event. Use your official CGM app and its alarm system for this purpose.

## No Medical Decision-Making

**Do not use GlucoTray as the basis for any medical decision**, including but not limited to insulin dosing, meal planning, or treatment of high or low blood glucose. Always consult your official CGM app, your prescribed medical devices, and your healthcare provider before making any treatment decisions.
